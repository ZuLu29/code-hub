Stay organized and keep track of deadlines and assignments.

•Use a planner or calendar to write down important dates, deadlines, and events.
•Set reminders for yourself to help you remember upcoming deadlines.
•Create a to-do list to prioritize your tasks and make sure you don't miss anything.
•Keep all of your course materials, such as notes, handouts, and assignments, in one place and organized.
•Use technology to your advantage, such as using a digital calendar or a task-management app.
•Take regular breaks and make sure to take care of yourself, both physically and mentally, to help you stay focused and on track.
•Regularly review your progress and adjust your plan as needed to stay on top of your work.
