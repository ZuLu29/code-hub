HOW TO SURVIVE THE BSIT COURSE

A Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) is a program that focuses on the study of technology, software, and computer systems used in businesses and organizations. It aims to prepare students to design, develop, and implement IT solutions to meet business needs. Surviving a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (IT) requires a combination of academic preparation, time management, and personal discipline.
